HOLDING COMPANY WEBSITE PROPOSAL
Scalable Corporate Platform (SEO + Marketing Optimized)
Date: 2026-06-13

Prepared for: Multi-sector Holding Company (Australia base, multi-country growth)
Baseline strategy reference: First Pacific structural discipline (not design copying)

====================================================================
1) EXECUTIVE PROPOSAL
====================================================================

Recommended approach:
- Build a high-performance, corporate-grade website using a headless architecture.
- Use Astro for the presentation layer and Sanity as the content platform.
- Deploy via edge CDN for speed, reliability, and SEO performance.

Why this approach:
- Faster load speed and better Core Web Vitals than heavy traditional CMS installs.
- Strong content governance and easy edits for non-technical staff.
- Structured content model for sectors, markets, leadership, and insights.
- Scales cleanly for future subsidiaries, countries, and multilingual rollout.

Primary strategic outcome:
A listed-style digital foundation that supports credibility, partnerships, business development,
and investor-grade communication across divisions.

====================================================================
3) PROPOSED STACK (NON-WORDPRESS)
====================================================================

Core stack:
- Frontend: Astro
- CMS: Sanity
- Hosting/CDN: Cloudflare Pages or Vercel
- Forms: Serverless/API endpoint + routing rules + anti-spam
- Analytics: GA4 + event schema

Why Astro over heavier alternatives:
- Ships less client-side JavaScript by default.
- Excellent static delivery for corporate content pages.
- Strong SEO control and predictable performance.

When to consider Next.js instead:
- If phase 2 requires heavy personalization, authenticated portals,
  or complex app-like behavior.

5) USER FLOW (HUMAN-READABLE)
====================================================================

A) Corporate discovery flow
1. Visitor lands on Home.
2. Understands parent company positioning and portfolio logic.
3. Chooses sector or corporate page.
4. Moves to relevant sector detail page.
5. Submits routed enquiry.

B) Partner / investor flow
1. Visitor reads About + Leadership & Governance.
2. Reviews Markets and portfolio logic.
3. Submits Partnerships/Investment contact route.
4. Internal team receives qualified enquiry by route.

C) Media / credibility flow
1. Visitor enters via News/Insights or search engine.
2. Reads thought leadership and sector relevance.
3. Navigates to sector page and Contact.

====================================================================

- Fast LCP and responsive UI on mobile + desktop.
- Optimized images and script minimization.

SEO:
- Correct heading structure and metadata per page.
- Sitemap and robots controls configured.
- Open Graph and basic structured data implemented.

- Editors can update core sections without layout breakage.
- New sector or market can be added via CMS model without redesign.

Governance:
- Stable, restrained brand execution.
- Corporate tone consistency across all pages.

====================================================================
10) RISKS AND CONTROLS
====================================================================

Risk: Overly promotional copy weakens credibility.
Control: Editorial style guide and content approval workflow.

Risk: Page builder sprawl and technical debt.
Control: Component-driven sections with strict schema.

Risk: Form spam and low-quality leads.
Control: Anti-spam, validation, and route rules.

Risk: Slow pages due to unoptimized media.
Control: Automated image pipeline and performance budgets.
