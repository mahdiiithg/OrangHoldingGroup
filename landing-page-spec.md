1. Sticky corporate header and responsive navigation
2. Hero with parent positioning and dual CTA
3. Group introduction plus approach card
4. Sector overview grid (all six sector cards)
5. Portfolio logic grouping section
6. Markets footprint section
7. News and insights preview cards
8. Key Corporate Events timeline preview
9. Strategic final CTA band
